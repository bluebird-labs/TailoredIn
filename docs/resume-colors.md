# Resume Color Guide for Executive Roles

Color recommendations for CTO, VP Engineering, and Head of Engineering resumes.

## Guiding Principle

At executive level, restraint signals authority. Color is an **accent**, not a feature. Apply it only to your name, section headings, or thin rule lines. Body text stays black on white.

Use **one accent color + black + white/light gray**. No more.

## Where to Apply Color

- **Name** at the top of the resume
- **Section headings** (Experience, Skills, Education)
- **Thin horizontal rules** or sidebar lines (optional)
- **Nothing else** -- bullets, body text, and dates stay black/dark gray

## Colors to Avoid

| Color | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Bright blue, green, orange, yellow | Too attention-seeking for executive level |
| Pure red (`#FF0000`) | Reads as aggressive |
| Multiple accent colors | One accent + black/gray is the ceiling |
| Pastels | Can read as unserious at this level |
